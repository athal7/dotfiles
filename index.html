Most of us share these values. Most of us also spend the day being driven — opening Slack, opening Linear, opening the AI chat, accepting the defaults.

This talk is about flipping that polarity.

Part II · The work loop

Who runs the how-loop?

Out of the loop
Hosted agents
Agents own the how-loop. PRs appear. e.g. Symphony, Codex, Devin.
On the loop
My pipeline
plan · TDD · review · commit · push. Agents do the work; I shape the harness.
Part II · The meta-work loop

Who shapes the workflow itself?

Out of the loop
The assistant reshapes itself
Vendor pushes updates. Skills change behind your back.
On the loop
Managed dotfiles
Skills, instructions, capability mappings — all files. Every change a commit I review.
Part II · Honest about

What being on the loop costs.

Parallel agents fracture attention beyond what engineering culture has language for.

So I borrowed language: monotropism, alexithymia, energy management. Built a small skill — attention — to give myself a buffer.

— with thanks to Neurodivergent Insights
